About Me

I am a data scientist with a solid foundation in finance, bringing a unique blend of analytical rigor, quantitative modeling, and business acumen to my work. With a degree in finance and several years of experience in the financial sector, I have honed my ability to derive meaningful insights from complex datasets, develop predictive models, and create data-driven strategies that drive business growth and efficiency. My journey into data science was fueled by a passion for uncovering patterns and stories hidden within data. I specialize in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualization, using tools like Python, SQL, and Tableau to transform raw data into actionable insights. I am skilled in developing algorithms for risk assessment, portfolio optimization, and financial forecasting, and I have experience working with large-scale financial datasets, including market data, transactional data, and customer behavior analytics.
